👻 Poke - Share quick photos with friends

Download Poke for Windows

Poke helps you snap and send quick pictures to your friends. Stay connected and share your daily moments with ease.

📱 About the App

Poke connects you with your friends through instant photo sharing. You capture a moment, send it, and stay in touch. The app focuses on speed and simplicity. You do not need to manage complicated settings or menus. Open the app, snap your photo, and select a friend to send it to. It keeps your social life simple and fun.

⚙️ System Requirements

  • Windows 10 or Windows 11
  • 4GB of RAM (8GB recommended)
  • A working webcam connected to your computer
  • Active internet connection for sending photos

📥 Installing Poke

Follow these steps to set up Poke on your Windows computer:

  1. Visit the Poke download page.
  2. Locate the latest version link on the page.
  3. Click the link to download the installer file to your computer.
  4. Open your Downloads folder and double-click the Poke installer file.
  5. Follow the instructions on the screen to finish the installation process.
  6. Once the installation completes, search for Poke in your Windows Start menu to open the app.

📸 How to Use Poke

Using Poke is straightforward. When you open the application, you see your camera feed immediately. Click the large capture button to take your photo. After you take the photo, the application prompts you to choose a friend from your list. Select the recipient and click send. Your friend receives the photo instantly.

🛡️ Privacy and Safety

Your privacy matters. Poke transmits your photos through a secure connection. You choose who sees your content. We do not track your activity beyond the necessary actions required to deliver your messages. Keep your account secure by using a strong password.

❓ Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, verify that your camera has permission to run. Go to your Windows Privacy Settings and ensure the camera access toggle is set to "On." If the app fails to open, restart your computer and try again. For further assistance, check the repository issues page for common solutions reported by other users.
